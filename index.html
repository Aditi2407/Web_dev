Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a Scottish wizarding school located in the Scottish Highlands.

Courses We Offer

All first-years at Hogwarts had to take seven core subjects: Transfiguration, Charms, Potions, History of Magic, Defence Against the Dark Arts, Astronomy and Herbology.[2] Flying lessons (on broomsticks) were also compulsory.[6] Flying was the only one of these that could be dropped upon entry into second year.
At the end of their second year at Hogwarts, students were required to choose a minimum of two additional subjects from the following list: Arithmancy, Muggle Studies, Divination, Study of Ancient Runes and Care of Magical Creatures.[7][1] Students couldn't elect to drop any of the core subjects at this time, but they could choose to drop an elective if it became a burden.
